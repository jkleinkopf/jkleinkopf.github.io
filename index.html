About

Joseph is a PhD student advised by Dr. Hannah Marx at The University of New Mexico and the Museum of Southwestern Biology Herbarium.

Research

I wonder why some plants thrive in the harsh environments of the alpine, while others do not. Also, when did alpine plants get here?

History

Previously, I taught 6th graders about earthquakes at Salish Middle School, studied the biogeography of a unique Hawaiian plant with Dr. Eric Roalson, and explored unique plants from the canyons of southeastern Colorado with Dr. Erin Tripp. See my CV for more.

Papers
